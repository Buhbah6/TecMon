# TecMon Design Guidelines

This document defines the required sprite set for each TecMon design.

## Core Requirements

- Every sprite must fit within a 64 x 64 pixel canvas.
- Each design needs two sprites:
	- Facing forward
	- Facing backward
- Keep the silhouette readable at small size.
- Use consistent proportions and visual language across all evolutions.

## Additional Sprite Sizes

- Items must fit within a 24 x 24 pixel space.
- Small creature sprites must fit within a 32 x 32 pixel space.

## Evolution Outline

The TecMon line follows this progression:

Base Form -> Evolved Form -> one of four third-form paths

The four third-form paths are:

- Bond Form
- Guardian Form
- Assist Form
- Power Form

## Required Designs

There are 6 total TecMon designs in this line:

1. Base Form
2. Evolved Form
3. Bond Form
4. Guardian Form
5. Assist Form
6. Power Form

Each of the 6 designs must have:

- 1 forward-facing sprite
- 1 backward-facing sprite

## Form Notes

### Base Form

- Smallest and simplest version of the TecMon.
- Establishes the core shape and identity.

### Evolved Form

- More developed than the Base Form.
- Should retain enough of the Base Form to be recognizable.
- Serves as the shared transition point for all third-form paths.

### Bond Form

- Special evolution available only for TecMon with high bond stats.
- Should feel emotionally connected or companion-like.
- All-Rounder build

### Guardian Form

- Defensive or protective specialization.
- Should communicate durability, shielding, or watchfulness.
- Tank Build

### Assist Form

- Support-focused specialization.
- Should feel helpful, precise, or utility-oriented.
- Status Effects & Healing Build

### Power Form

- Offensive or high-force specialization.
- Should communicate strength, impact, or intensity.
- DPS Build

## Production Checklist

- Base Form: forward and backward sprites complete
- Evolved Form: forward and backward sprites complete
- Bond Form: forward and backward sprites complete
- Guardian Form: forward and backward sprites complete
- Assist Form: forward and backward sprites complete
- Power Form: forward and backward sprites complete
- All sprites verified at 64 x 64 pixels or smaller
